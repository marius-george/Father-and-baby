Pizza with Whipped Ricotta & Roasted Cherry Tomatoes

This super easy Pizza with Whipped Ricotta & Roasted Cherry Tomatoes will be a new family favourite. Making pizza bases is much easier than you may think, and a fun activity to get the kids involved with too. Top with soft ricotta & Piccolo Cherry Tomatoes for a delicious pizza night in!

Ingredients

300g strong white bread flour
1 teaspoon dried yeast
1 tablesoon olive oil
200mls water
1/2 teaspoon salt

4 x 220g packet of Piccolo Cherry Tomatoes
4 cloves garlic, sliced
5 sprigs thyme
2 tablespoons olive oil
bunch basil

250g ricotta cheese
250g mozzarella ball, chopped
50g parmesan cheese, grated

Method

  1. Make the dough. Measure the flour, yeast, oil, water and salt into a bowl. Mix together to form a sticky dough. Knead on a floured work surface until smooth. Put into an oiled bowl, cover and leave to rise for 1 hour.
  2. Pre heat the oven to 220 Fan. Divide the dough into three pieces. Roll the dough out into 3 small oval shapes. Place on a large baking sheet line with baking paper.
  3. Put the tomatoes, garlic, thyme and oil onto a baking sheet. Put both trays into the oven for 12-15 minutes.
  4. Measure the ricotta, mozzarella and seasoning into a bowl. Mash together.
  5. Leave the bases to cool slightly, then spread over the ricotta mixture. Top with the tomatoes, basil and parmesan cheese.
